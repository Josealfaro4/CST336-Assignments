The myDoom virus was a computer virus that only affected Microsoft based Computers.

APPLE FOR THE WIN !!!

This virus was sent through an email and the virus downloaded as soon as the email was opened.

This virus can also be identified as W32.MyDoom@mm, Novag, Mimail.R and Shimgapi.
